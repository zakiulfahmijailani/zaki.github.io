Background

About

My work sits at the intersection of geographic information systems, large language models, and public policy — particularly in the context of Indonesia's urban and regional development. Currently developing SIGAPP, an agentic geospatial AI system for education planning in Nusa Tenggara Timur (NTT).

Research Interests

  • Geographic Vector Embeddings & spatial knowledge graphs

  • PostGIS + LLM integration for spatial operations
  • Education policy analytics with geospatial context
